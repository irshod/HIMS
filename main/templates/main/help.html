Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center for HIMS. Below, you'll find instructions for common tasks and workflows in the system.

Common Workflows

  • How to register a new patient?

    If this is the patient's first visit, navigate to the Patient Module, click "Register Patient", and fill in the necessary details like name, date of birth, gender, contact information, and address.

  • How to create an appointment for an existing patient?

    Search for the patient in the Patient List. If found, go to their profile and click "Create Appointment". Choose the department, doctor, and service, and confirm the appointment.

  • How to admit a patient to IPD?

    If a patient requires admission, navigate to the IPD Module. Search for the patient, select "Admit", choose the room, bed, and admission details, and confirm the admission.

  • How to add medication for an admitted patient?

    Go to the patientâ€™s IPD profile. Under the "Medications" tab, click "Add Medication". Choose the medication, dosage, and duration, then save the record.

  • How to view a patient's history?

    To view the complete history of a patient, search for the patient in the Patient Module, go to their profile, and navigate to the "History" tab. Here, you can see past appointments, treatments, and medications.

System Instructions

  • How to update your profile?

    Navigate to "View Profile" from the dropdown menu and click "Edit" to update your details.

  • How to manage inventory?

    Go to the Inventory Module to add, edit, or update medications and consumables. You can also set reorder thresholds and track stock levels.

  • How to generate invoices?

    In the Finance Module, you can generate invoices for appointments, admissions, and other services. Go to the respective section, click "Generate Invoice", and confirm payment details.

  • How to create departments and assign staff?

    ONLY ADMIN!!!
    Navigate to the Departments Module, click "Add Department", and fill in the details. Assign doctors and nurses to the department and specify the services offered.
